Jugaadoo is an independent film project made with the purpose of inspiring Indian youth.

The Journey

Three friends set out on a journey 5,000 miles across India. 2.5 months. These are the stories of the people they met. The method: improvisation. This is where it all began.

The Vision

More than a documentary, this is a project pushing forward with innovation to show what innovation is, both abstract and physical. It is minds at work searching for ways to improve their communities, whether on a local scale or a much bigger one. Even beyond the actual ideas and inventions and organizations highlighted, the documentary and the project are evidence of the spirit of the community, the innate desire to be a part of something bigger than ourselves. With that comes possibility. We all believe in possibilty and, more importantly, want to believe in possibility and a sense of boundlessness. That is not to say innovation is some giant movement all the time—most things start small, and if people see the truth and honesty in what is being done, it is sure to grow. We learn from each other—education is an exchange, it is actively looking to others for knowledge you don't have; it is not, and cannot be, an introverted process.
